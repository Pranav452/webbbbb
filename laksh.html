About Ingole's Physiotherapy

At Ingole's Physiotherapy, we are dedicated to providing high-quality, personalized care to help you achieve optimal physical health and well-being. Our team of experienced physiotherapists uses evidence-based techniques and state-of-the-art equipment to address a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions and injuries.

With years of experience and a passion for helping people, we have become a trusted name in physiotherapy in Aurangabad. Our holistic approach to treatment ensures that we not only address your immediate concerns but also work towards preventing future issues.

Have more questions? We've got you!

How do you ensure consistent quality across sessions?

We maintain strict quality standards and provide ongoing training to our physiotherapists to ensure consistent, high-quality care for all our patients.

Do you offer specialized treatments for athletes?

Yes, we have specialized sports physiotherapy programs designed to cater to the unique needs of athletes and help them recover from sports-related injuries.

What if I'm not happy with my bundle?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your treatment, we'll work with you to address your concerns or offer a refund for unused sessions.

How long does a typical physiotherapy session last?

Our standard physiotherapy sessions typically last 45-60 minutes, depending on the treatment plan and individual needs of the patient.

Do you provide home visits for physiotherapy sessions?

Yes, we offer home visit services for patients who are unable to come to our clinic. Please contact us for more information on home visit availability and pricing.
